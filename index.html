Top three places you should visit at Kunming

Enjoy nice walk in Green Lake Park

Take a peaceful walk through Green Lake Park, surrounded by fresh air, shimmering water, and lush greenery. Stroll along the lakeside, feel the breeze, and soak in the soothing sounds of nature.

Have BBQ party with friends by Dian Lake

Gather with friends by Dian Lake for a BBQ, enjoying great food, laughter, and scenic views. Relax by the water, share stories, and make the most of the beautiful lakeside setting.

Try speical Yunnan cusine at historical district

Taste unique Yunnan flavors in the historic district, enjoying rich dishes amid charming old architecture
